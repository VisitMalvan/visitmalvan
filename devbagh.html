Why Visit Devbagh?

Devbagh is famous for its beautiful beach, the meeting point of the Karli River and Arabian Sea, dolphin sightings and exciting water sports.

Located just a few kilometres from Malvan, Devbagh offers peaceful surroundings and unforgettable experiences for couples, families and adventure lovers.
